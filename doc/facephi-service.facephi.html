facephi-service.facephi

Wraps the FacePhi Matcher SDK.

authenticate

Returns true or false whether the faces was authenticated or not.

authentication-matcher-configuration

auto-retrain

(auto-retrain existing-face new-face)

authentication.

(auto-retrain-matcher-configuration)
